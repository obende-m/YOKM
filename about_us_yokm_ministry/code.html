A Call to Compassion

Yendel Ocha Kpeling Ministry was born from a profound realization that spiritual support alone cannot sustain a family in crisis. In 2015, observing the plight of widows in rural communities—women who were often stripped of their property and social status—the foundation was laid to provide both a spiritual home and tangible economic relief.

Expanding the Reach

What started as a small prayer group in a local living room has evolved into a registered NGO serving five regional districts with holistic aid programs.

Sustainable Impact

We believe in hand-ups, not just hand-outs. Our strategy focuses on vocational training and micro-loans to ensure that every beneficiary finds a path to independence.

The Heart Behind the Mission

Evangelist Alice ("Mama Alice")

format_quote
"Jesus is their husband, but they also need hope, healing and food."

Known affectionately by the community as 'Mama Alice,' Evangelist Alice founded YOKM with a vision to be the physical hands and feet of Christ. Having seen the struggles of the marginalized firsthand, she has dedicated her life to ensuring no widow feels abandoned and no child goes to bed hungry.
